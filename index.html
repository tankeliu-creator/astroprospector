About Astro Prospector

Astro Prospector is an engaging space mining adventure that combines resource management with exciting gameplay. As a space prospector, you'll explore various celestial bodies, mine valuable resources, and upgrade your spacecraft to become the ultimate space explorer. The game features intuitive controls, progressive difficulty, and a rewarding upgrade system that keeps you engaged as you journey through the cosmos.

How It Works

AstroProspector combines resource management with space exploration in an engaging gameplay loop:

  1. Start with basic mining equipment and explore nearby asteroids
  2. Extract resources while managing oxygen and equipment condition
  3. Sell resources to upgrade equipment and expand operations
  4. Build and maintain bases to process resources more efficiently
  5. Research new technologies to access more valuable resources
  6. Expand your mining empire across different space sectors

Risk Factors

  • • Manage risks like oxygen depletion, equipment damage, geological hazards, and hostile alien encounters
  • • Players must constantly manage risks and improve survival capabilities

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the system requirements?

AstroProspector runs in any modern web browser with JavaScript enabled. No additional software installation is required.

Is there a tutorial for beginners?

Yes, the game includes an interactive tutorial that teaches you the basics of mining, resource management, and base construction.

Can I save my progress?

Your progress is automatically saved in your browser. You can continue where you left off when you return to the game.

How do I handle environmental hazards?

Monitor your oxygen levels, maintain your equipment, and upgrade your protective systems to handle various space hazards like radiation and extreme temperatures.
